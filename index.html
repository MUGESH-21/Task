Grandma's Secret Apple pie


This apple pie recipe has been in my family for generation and never fails to delight.


Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 6 medium-sized aooles,peeled and sliced

Instruction

  1. Preheat oven to 375`f(190`c)
  2. In a bowel,mix the sugar and cinnamon.
  3. Toss the apples in the sugar-cinnamon mixture
